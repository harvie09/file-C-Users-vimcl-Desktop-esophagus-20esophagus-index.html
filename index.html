About Us

A food navigation website that would mostly serve students at City College of Calamba, especially freshmen who are unfamiliar with the place, as most of the students are still unfamiliar with the locations of food vendors.

Our goal is to establish a website that displays a variety of meals and the closest area where they can be purchased. This website provides the location of the store near CCC, prices of products, and recommendations that can save time and money. This also has a big impact on food vendors since this website can also be used as an advertisement, therefore assisting students and food merchants.
